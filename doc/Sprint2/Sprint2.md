---

### Meetings and Events  

To maintain alignment and efficiency, we will conduct the following meetings and events:  

- **Review Meeting:**  
  - **Time:** Fridays, 3 - 5 PM (Online)  
  - Purpose: Allows the team to review the progress of the project, present completed work, and discuss any issues or challenges faced during the sprint.  
  - This meeting ensures alignment on what’s working, what needs improvement, and setting goals for the next sprint.  

- **RPMs (Review, Planning, and Management):**  
  - **Time:** Tuesdays, 1 - 2 PM (Online)  
  - Purpose: A formal check-in to ensure that progress aligns with the initial goals and discuss any changes in scope or resources.  
  - Helps the team align on what needs to be planned for the upcoming week.  

- **Standups:**  
  - **Time:** Weekdays, 4 - 4:15 PM (Online)  
  - Purpose: A quick check-in to track the progress of individual tasks, raise potential blockers, and align on daily priorities.  

---

### Task Management and Organization  

To maintain structure and accountability, we will utilize the following artifacts:  

- **Task Board:** Used to track ongoing tasks, assignments, and progress.  
- **To-Do Lists:** Created for individual members to manage their short-term priorities.  
- **Weekly Task Prioritization:** Tasks will be assigned based on urgency and dependencies to ensure an optimal workflow.
